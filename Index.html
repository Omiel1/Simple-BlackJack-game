    Easy Blackjack - Quick rules:
  • Your goal is to get your card score as close to 21 as possible!
  • Players start with 2 cards and can draw up to 3 more cards.
  • There is a total of 12 cards in deck with values between 1 and 12.
  • Players lose if they go over the score of 21.
  • The round concludes when both player decide to stand
  • Players can't see other players cards
